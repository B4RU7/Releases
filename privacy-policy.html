Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how our app uses and protects user data. By using our app, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Data Collection

Our app does not collect any personal information or usage data from users, except for the data that is required to provide the camera functionality and save images to a private Firestore database that is accessible by both the client and the developers/administrators.

Camera Permissions

Our app uses the android.permission.CAMERA permission to allow you to take pictures or videos within the app. This permission is used solely for the purpose of enabling the camera functionality within our app, and no images or videos are collected or stored by our app without your explicit consent.

Storage Permissions

Our app uses the android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permissions to allow you to save images to a private Firestore database that is accessible by both the client and the developers/administrators. These permissions are used solely for the purpose of enabling the storage functionality within our app, and no data is collected or stored by our app without your explicit consent.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored on your device and in our private Firestore database from unauthorized access or disclosure, and to ensure the integrity and availability of your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not display any ads, nor does it use any third-party services that may collect or use your data.

Your Choices

Our app does not collect any personal information or usage data from users, except for the data that is required to provide the camera and storage functionality within our app. You may choose to uninstall our app at any time if you no longer wish to use it.
